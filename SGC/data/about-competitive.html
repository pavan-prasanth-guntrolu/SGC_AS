Competitive Club

About Us

This club is for members to collaborate on exam preparation, share their expertise and experiences, and receive mentorship and support.

Objectives

  • To help members prepare for various competitive exams such as GRE, GMAT, CAT,UPSC and GATE.
  • Provide a platform for members to prepare for and excel in competitive exams, such as national and international aptitude tests, entrance exams, and qualifying exams.
  • Foster a culture of academic excellence and achievement by promoting learning and practice in various subjects, such as mathematics, physics, chemistry, biology, English, and general knowledge.
