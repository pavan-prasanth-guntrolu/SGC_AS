Photography club

About Us

Provide an environment for members to share and learn about photography and related subjects.

Objectives

  • Develop partnerships with local organizations and events to provide members with opportunities to photograph and document community events and initiatives.
  • Encourage members to take on leadership roles within the club, such as event planning or leading workshops, to develop their communication and leadership skills
  • Promote the ethical and legal aspects of photography, including copyright, privacy, and representation.
