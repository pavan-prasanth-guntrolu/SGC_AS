About Us

Provide a platform for members to learn about and appreciate different aspects of automobile technology, design, and engineering, such as car mechanics, engine tuning, and vehicle safety.

Objectives

  • Host automobile competitions, and design challenges to provide members with opportunities to showcase their skills, collaborate with others, and gain recognition.
  • Create a platform for members to collaborate on automobile projects, share their expertise and experiences, and receive mentorship and support.
  • Develop partnerships with local automobile companies, dealerships, and racing clubs to provide members with access to automobile resources, events, and experiences.
