About Us

Provide opportunities for members to collaborate on electronics projects, share resources, and exchange ideas..

Objectives

  • Develop partnerships with local businesses, universities, and organizations to provide members with access to advanced electronics tools and resources.
  • Foster a culture of innovation and creativity by encouraging members to experiment with new technologies and push the boundaries of what is possible.
  • Provide mentorship and support to members who are interested in pursuing careers in electronics and related fields.
