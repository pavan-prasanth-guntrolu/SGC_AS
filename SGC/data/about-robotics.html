Robotics Club

About Us

Provide an avenue for members to learn about and build robots and related technologies.

Objectives

  • Encourage members to take on leadership roles within the club, such as project management or event planning, to develop their communication and leadership skills.
  • Collaborate with other organizations and clubs to provide members with access to a broader range of resources, such as mentors and industry contacts.
  • Promote the ethical and social aspects of robotics, including the impact of robotics on society, privacy, and safety.
