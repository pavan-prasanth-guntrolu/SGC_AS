Arts & Crafts Club

About Us

Provide a space for members to explore and express their creativity through various forms of arts and crafts.

Objectives

  • Host exhibitions and showcases to provide members with opportunities to display their work and receive feedback from peers and experts.
  • Create a platform for members to collaborate on projects, share their expertise and experiences, and receive mentorship and support.
  • Encourage members to take on leadership roles within the club, such as project management or event planning, to develop their communication and leadership skills.
