About Us

Promote physical fitness and well-being among the members of the club.

Objectives

  • Develop a culture of fair play, respect for rules, and discipline in sports and games.
  • Promote healthy competition and friendly rivalry among members and other clubs.
  • Engage in community service by organizing sports events for underprivileged children and promoting physical activity in the local community.
