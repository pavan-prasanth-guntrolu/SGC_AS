Undergrad Research club

About Us

Provide an environment for members to explore and develop their ideas for new businesses, products, or services.

Activities

  • Develop partnerships with local institutions and organizations to provide members with access to business incubators, mentors, and funding opportunities.
  • Encourage members to take on leadership roles within the club, such as project management or event planning, to develop their communication and leadership skills.
  • Collaborate with other organizations and clubs to provide members with access to a broader range of resources, such as industry contacts and legal advice.
